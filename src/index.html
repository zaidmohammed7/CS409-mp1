About

At Deccan Dawat, authenticity matters. We slow-cook, hand-grind, and layer flavors with the same patience that has defined Hyderabadi cooking for centuries. Each dish is carefully prepared to highlight the depth and richness of our culinary heritage.

From the first sip of Irani chai to the last bite of Double ka Meetha, we bring the city’s spirit to your plate.

Slow Dum

Sealed pots and gentle heat deepen flavor while rice stays fragrant and separate.

Fresh Masalas

Festive Finish

From haleem to Double ka Meetha, every plate feels like a celebration.
